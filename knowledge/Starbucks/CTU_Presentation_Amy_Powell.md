##### Starbucks® Roast


- The Starbucks® Roast is not a time, temperature or color – it’s a

philosophy.


- Our coffee beans realize their full potential only through careful

batch roasting – a process monitored and adjusted constantly by
our skilled roasting experts.


- Our tradition is built on an artisanal approach of hand selecting,

roasting and blending each individual recipe.
