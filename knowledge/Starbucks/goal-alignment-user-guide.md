### **System Navigation**

##### **Log On**


1. Open your browser and navigate to https://performancemanager4.successfactors.com/login
2. Enter **Starbucks** in the **Company Id:** field
3. Enter your case‐sensitive Username and Password
4. Click **Log in**

##### **Forgot Your Password?**


1. Open your browser and navigate to https://performancemanager4.successfactors.com/login
2. Click the **Forgot Your Password?** link
3. Enter **Starbucks** in the **Company Id:** field
4. Enter your Username in the **Username:** field

5. Click **Submit**

##### **Navigation Basics**


1. Upon logging in, the **Home** page displays. This page provides access to all the activities and processes in the
system, and gives an overview of your To‐Dos. To access **Home** from anywhere in the application, select **Home**
from the drop‐down menu at the upper left‐hand side of the screen.


2. Use the module drop‐down menu to go different parts of the system. Select a module from the list to move.
Partners may select the following modules:


    - Home


    - Goals


    - Performance


    - Company Info


    - Partner Profile


Managers may access additional modules based upon their role.


3. Click the arrow next to your name on the upper right‐hand side of the screen to access the **Options** menu
and to **Logout** of the system.


4. Use People Search to locate other partners. Search uses predictive text that allows partial entries to assist
with locating an individual. Search is accessible from any location within the system.

##### **The Browser Back Button**


**NOTE: Please refrain from using the Back Button.**

The Starbucks Talent Management System is designed to keep your personal data confidential. To do this, the
website does not allow the use of the Back Button. If you click the Back Button, you may lose data and may need
to log back into the system.

### **Overview of SuccessFactors Modules**


SuccessFactors is made up of online modules that automate Starbucks Goal Alignment, Partner Development
Planning, Prganization and Partner Planning (OPP) and Annual Review processes. Below are short descriptions
of each module in the Starbucks system.

#### **Goals**


An online plan used to create and store your annual goals in a central place.

#### **Performance**


An online annual review form used to record partner accomplishments, strengths and opportunities. These
forms are organized in a series of folders to keep each year’s review in easy reach.

    - **Inbox:** Forms that require your action.


    - **En Route:** Forms that require actions from other people.


    - **Completed:** Forms that are complete are stored here.

#### **Development**


An online form used to document your development plan.

#### **Company Info**


**Company Info** is where company specific materials and information is posted. Tools include: **Org Chart**,
**Directory** and **Resources.**

#### **Partner Profile**


This is your “online resume” which is accessible to you, your manager, and Partner Resources. Your profile lists
your work experience, education and qualifications.

**NOTE:** It is your responsibility to keep your profile up‐to‐date because it is used for talent planning purposes.

### **Goals: Cascade Goals**


Goals can be aligned by cascading them from your goal plan to other partners. Cascading a goal means sharing one of
the your goals with another patner within the hierarchy. Managers cascade their goals to individual partners to align the
team’s goals with starbucks strategy.

#### **Manager Action: Cascade Goals To Direct Reports**


1. On the **Home** page, click **Goals** from the drop‐down in the upper left‐hand corner.


2. Click the check box to the left of the goal to cascade and click the button.


3. Clickthe check box next to each partner that needs the selected goal on their goal plan.


4. Click, then edit Goal and Measure fields, as necessary.


5. Click **.**


**Note:** Edits only appear in the recipient’s Goal Plan, the original goal remains unchanged.

#### **Manager Action: Cascade Goals to an Organiztion or Function**


1. On the **Home** page, click **Goals** from the drop‐down in the upper left‐hand corner.


2. Click the check box to the left of the goal to cascade and click the button.


3. Click the view icon to the left of a partners who manage others.


4. Click the check box next to each partner that needs the selected goal added to their goal plan.


5. Repeat Step 3 and 4 until all selections are made.


**Note:** As you repeat step 3 and 4, the list of selected partners updates automatically.


Click to use a search form to find partners.


6. Click, then edit the Goal and Measure fields as necessary.


7. Click **.**
